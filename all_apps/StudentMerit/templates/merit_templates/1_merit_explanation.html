What is a Merit System?

A student has 100 points at the beginning of the academic year. Upon completion of a certain merit action, a student receives points, and the opposite happens if the demerit action is commited. This system is used to motivate students to actively participate in academic, sport and other activities of the school life. At the same time, it discourages students to misbehave. Depending on the school policy, a certain points thresholds exist for rewarding or punishing actions.
